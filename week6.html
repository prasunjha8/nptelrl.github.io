1. Temporal Difference (TD) Learning & TD(0)

TD combines Monte Carlo and Dynamic Programming. It learns from experience and bootstraps.

TD(0) Update

V(s_t) ← V(s_t) + α [R_{t+1} + γV(s_{t+1}) - V(s_t)]

TD Error:

δ = R_{t+1} + γV(s_{t+1}) - V(s_t)
